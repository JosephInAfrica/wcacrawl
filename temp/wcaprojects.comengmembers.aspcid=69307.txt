<div class="memberprofile_row memberprofile_detail">Final Mile Logistics, Inc. (FML) is a global supply chain logistics provider based in Atlanta, Georgia. We provide a full range of logistics services to US Government departments and agencies, and to government contractors and commercial companies both domestically and outside the continental United States.<br>
<br>
Final Mile Logistics' project cargo capabilities include heavy lift, out-of-gauge, break-bulk, and high value cargo moves. Final Mile has experience moving an extensive variety of project cargo, including military vehicles, mining equipment, flight simulators, generators, and construction materials; many times delivering into extremely hard to reach and austere destinations. At Final Mile, we are dedicated to take on any challenge within our capabilities, no matter how daunting.<br>
<br>
Final Mile Logistics has solid and proven distribution platforms globally and has a history of successfully delivering services into some of the hardest to reach and volatile destinations in the world. FML's core competencies include all modes of transportation and freight management, project cargo, warehousing, distribution, air charter services, customs brokerage, in-transit visibility solutions and private security escorts for convoys moving in hostile regions.<br>
<br>
Our seasoned and experienced professional staff provides end-to-end supply chain solutions, project logistics and project management. We have fully engaged warehouse operations in strategic global locations, where we sort and consolidate inbound freight, load according to workload schedules and deliver within agreed upon times. Our record for on time deliveries without discrepancies is exceptional.<br>
<br>
We understand that no two customers are the same and that they are all too often pushed into solutions where their specific wants and needs are ignored. Final Mile Logistics is dedicated to providing logistics solutions to the daily challenges faced when doing business. We continue to solidify existing networks and work on building new domestic and international relationships in order to consistently deliver our 'Final Mile Peace of Mind' guarantee: To take the logistics burden out of our customers hands allowing them to focus on the needs of their businesses.</div>
